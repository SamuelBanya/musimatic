Places To Visit

This is a webpage that features the coolest places to visit in Monaco.

Architecture

Monte Carlo Casino

This is the gambling and entertainment complex, which includes the casino, the Opéra de Monte-Carlo, and the office of Les Ballets de Monte Carlo.

Palais du Prince

This is the home of the oldest monarchy in the world. This was originally built in the 13th century as a Genoese fortress, and offers panoramic views of the city.

Museums

Musée Oceanographique

Sports

Monaco Grand Prix

This is the annual racing event held in Monaco, which has been taking place since 1929, and features one of the most demanding tracks in Formula One racing.
