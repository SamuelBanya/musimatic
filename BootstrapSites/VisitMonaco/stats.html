Geography

Location

Monaco is located on the French Riviera in Western Europe.

Boroughs

Monaco is a city state that consists of five quartiers, and ten wards.

Population

Demographics

Monaco has a population of 37,550 people, as of 12/13/2016

The largest group are French nationals at 28.4%, followed by Monégasque (21.6%), Italian (18.7%), British (7.5%), Belgian (2.8%), German (2.5%), Swiss (2.5%) and U.S. nationals (1.2%).

Language

The official language of Monaco is French, while Italian is also spoken.
